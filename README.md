# Bobot_2_Amethyst
Second robot from Bobot series of robots. A huge amount of things in this robot address problems of previous Bobot.

# Features 

## Hardware

### Handle

On the top there will be handle which will be hidden in base.
It would rotate out on bearings.
Rotation will be locked by mechanism on top of base.

### Back buttons

On the back there will be 9 buttons to interact with bobot.
They will be with 3d printed tops with symbols representing purpose.

Buttons:
```
 _________________________
| Settings |  Up  |  Text |
|__________|______|_______|
|   Left   |  OK  | Right |
|__________|______|_______|
| Text     | Down |  Text |
|__________|______|_______|
```

### Power switch

At the bottom of left side of bobot(looking at their face) there will be power switch, which will connect batteries to power regulation module.
